About Us

ACM GEU is a student chapter of the Association of Computing Machinery. Students run our student organization at Graphic Era Deemed to be University (GEU), which is dedicated to finding ourselves and our passions, meeting new people, learning industrial skills to enhance ourselves, learning about technology and research, helping our community, being inspired and changing the world!!!
